Use this when the active BlueprintLoop is ready for audit or cannot continue.

Execution agents may set:
- auditing: the implementation is ready for audit
- failed: execution cannot continue

Audit agents may set:
- completed: audit passed
- failed: audit cannot continue

Parameters:
- loopID: The active BlueprintLoop ID
- status: "auditing", "failed", or "completed" according to the role rules above
- summary: Optional completion, failure, or audit summary
