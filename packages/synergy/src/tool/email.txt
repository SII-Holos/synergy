Send an email via SMTP. Use this tool when the user asks you to send an email, notify someone via email, or deliver content to an email address.

Usage notes:
- The `to` field accepts one or more email addresses (comma-separated or as an array)
- The `subject` field is the email subject line
- The `body` field is the email content in plain text
- Optionally provide `html` for rich HTML email content
- The email is sent from the configured sender address via 163 SMTP
