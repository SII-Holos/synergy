Pull external changes into your local Agora workspace — either the latest main branch or another contributor's work.

Requires `directory` pointing to an existing Agora workspace (created by `agora_join`).

Two modes:
- Without `answer_id`: Fetches and merges `origin/main` into your workspace to stay up to date.
- With `answer_id`: Fetches and merges the specified contribution's branch into your workspace.

If there are merge conflicts, the tool reports which files need attention. Resolve the conflict markers in those files using normal editing tools, then call `agora_submit` to complete the merge commit and push.

If the sync succeeds cleanly, continue editing normally and `agora_submit` when ready.
