Set a one-time wake-up in THIS session. Use when you need to pause and come back later — e.g. wait for a deploy, let a process run, or check results after a delay.

When the time comes, you are woken up in this session with the prompt as your instruction. No new session is created. The watch auto-completes after firing.

Examples:
- "Check pipeline health in 30 minutes": agenda_watch(title="Check pipeline", delay="30m", prompt="Check if...")
- "Revisit in 2 hours": agenda_watch(title="Revisit analysis", delay="2h", prompt="Continue the analysis...")

After the watch fires, you continue working in this session with full conversation history.

Use agenda_list to see active watches. Use agenda_cancel(id) to cancel before it fires.
If you need a RECURRING task (cron, every), use agenda_schedule instead.
