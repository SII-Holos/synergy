Use this when the BlueprintLoop review finds missing, incorrect, or unverified required work.

Parameters:
- sessionID: The execution session ID provided in your launch context
- reason: Clear explanation of why the Blueprint outcome is not complete
- completed: Optional summary of work that is already correct
- remaining: Missing or incorrect work, marking each item BLOCKING or NON-BLOCKING
- instructions: Concrete next actions the execution agent can follow without further clarification
